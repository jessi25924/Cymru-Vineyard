Vineyard Tour

Includes:

  • Guided Vineyard Walk: A stroll through the vineyards with explanations about grape varieties, cultivation practices, and the winemaking process. You may also learn about the vineyard's history and the region's wine traditions.

  • Wine Tasting Experience: A tasting session of the vineyard's wines, often led by a sommelier or expert. This may include learning how to properly taste wine, identifying flavors, and pairing suggestions.

  • Cellar Tour and Production Insight: A behind-the-scenes look at the wine production process, including visits to the fermentation rooms, barrel storage, and bottling areas. Some tours may also include demonstrations or hands-on activities like grape crushing (seasonal).
